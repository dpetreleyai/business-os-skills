# Weekly Review

Fifteen minutes, five questions, one dated note. The point is the writing:
a business that reviews weekly compounds.

## Procedure

1. Ask, one at a time (short answers welcome):
   - **Wins** — what moved forward this week?
   - **Numbers** — the 1-3 figures that matter (revenue, leads, published,
     shipped — whatever the interview said this business runs on).
   - **Stuck** — what stalled, and is it blocked-on-you, blocked-on-them, or
     blocked-on-unknown?
   - **Drop** — anything to stop doing?
   - **Three priorities** for next week (exactly three; force the cut).
2. Write `reference/reviews/YYYY-MM-DD-weekly.md` at the business root (create
   the folder on first run) with those five sections, verbatim where possible.
3. Link it: add/update a `[[weekly-review]]`-tagged line in the vault
   `MEMORY.md` dashboard pointing at the latest review.
4. If a stuck item names a department, offer ONE next action for it — a task
   the owner could launch from that department's button right now.
5. Close by reading back the three priorities in one line.

## Rules

- 15 minutes means 15 minutes: no follow-up interrogation, no strategy essay.
- Numbers the owner doesn't know are answered "don't know" — record that too;
  three "don't know"s in a row is itself a finding worth flagging.
- Reviews are append-only history. Never edit a past week's note.
